How It Works

Our simple, transparent 5-step publication journey

01

Free Initial Consultation

Reach out via email or WhatsApp. We understand your research topic, current manuscript status, indexing goals, and budget to recommend the ideal service package.

02

Manuscript Preparation

Our writers/editors craft or refine your manuscript — ensuring coherent structure, original language, plagiarism-free content, and compliance with chosen journal guidelines.

03

Journal Shortlisting & Your Approval

We present 3–5 curated journals matching your research scope and indexing requirements. You review and approve the target journal before submission.

04

Submission & Review Management

We format and submit your paper, then manage all communication with the journal. Reviewer responses are handled by our experts with precision and clarity.

05

Acceptance & APC Payment

Upon acceptance, we notify you and coordinate the APC payment directly with the journal (no commission added). We then share the published DOI and proof.
